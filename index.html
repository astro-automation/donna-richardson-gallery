The Human Behind the Work

Spirit. Memory. Color. Presence.

Donna’s work is not only viewed — it is felt. Her world carries joy, legacy, cultural memory, reflection, humor, spiritual openness, and a deep relationship with beauty. This digital gallery is designed to make the viewer feel the emotional atmosphere before they ever enter a single series.
