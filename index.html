Meet Donna

Donna Richardson’s work explores emotional memory, Black cultural storytelling, spiritual symbolism, feminine energy, rhythm, joy, ancestry, and human presence through immersive contemporary portraiture and mixed media expression.

“Art is spirit. Art is memory. Art is emotional truth.”
