Seven Series

A living archive of color, culture & presence

Each body of work becomes its own room inside the digital exhibition — a guided pathway through Donna’s subjects, palette, memory, rhythm, and emotional power.

Series I

Children in light

A radiant body of work honoring childhood, memory, innocence, and the emotional presence of young lives rendered with dignity and tenderness.

Inquire About This Series →
Series II

Sports in motion

The athlete becomes architecture, spirit, discipline, and movement — figures suspended between effort, grace, and victory.

Inquire About This Series →
Series III

Music made visible

Sound becomes pigment. Rhythm becomes brushstroke. Donna’s music works translate performance and cultural memory into visual movement.

Inquire About This Series →
Series IV

Icons and witnesses

A reverent meditation on figures who shaped culture, memory, movement, excellence, and public imagination.

Inquire About This Series →
Series V

Cigar and ritual

Smoke, leisure, quiet authority, and the visual language of stillness become a sophisticated meditation on time and presence.

Inquire About This Series →
Series VI

Faces & Figures

Portraiture as inquiry — each face, body, and figure carrying a story of identity, dignity, emotion, and human complexity.

Series VII

Nature as cathedral

The natural world becomes a sacred visual space — sky, water, earth, quietness, and the wonder of what holds us.
