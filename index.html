— The Seven Series

Bodies of Work

Each series is its own room, its own moment, its own meditation. Together they form the living archive of Donna M. Richardson’s vision.

Series I

Children — in Light

A radiant study of innocence. Each work captures a fleeting moment — the unguarded smile, the sleeping breath, the hand reaching upward — rendered in lavender, cream, and quiet gold.

Inquire About This Series →
Series II

Sports — Bodies in Motion

The athlete as architecture. Donna paints the geometry of effort — taut muscle, suspended breath, and the moment before triumph — in cobalt blue and electric fuchsia.

Inquire About This Series →
Series III

Music — Sound Made Visible

From the smoky hush of jazz clubs to the brass shout of festival stages, this series translates rhythm into pigment — turquoise, ocean blue, and deep horn-gold.

Inquire About This Series →
Series IV

Icons — Cultural Witnesses

A reverent reframing of the figures who shaped a generation. Donna paints them not as celebrities, but as witnesses — weighty, dignified, and unshakeable.

Inquire About This Series →
Series V

Cigar — A Quiet Authority

Smoke as gesture. Leisure as language. This intimate series treats the cigar ritual as meditation — on time, taste, and the quiet authority of considered presence.

Inquire About This Series →
Series VI

Faces & Figures

Portraiture as inquiry. Each face is a question, each figure a quiet answer — bound together by Donna’s empathic, unflinching, deeply human eye.

Inquire About This Series →
Series VII

Nature — What Holds Us

The natural world as cathedral. Lavender skies. Ocean horizons. The quiet majesty of place — Donna’s tribute to what surrounds, sustains, and ultimately holds us.
